UX Research

OTT Binge Watching Phenomenon in Generation Z :
Design Responsibility and Dark Patterns in UX

Current practices in the field of user experience of OTT Platforms suggest that a lot of dark patterns exist in the design for increased engagement which significantly affect the productivity of generation Z who are the most avid users of the platforms.

The purpose of this research is to explore how UX design, especially dark patterns, influences the binge-watching habits of Generation Z on OTT platforms, while examining the ethical responsibilities of designers in creating user-friendly and responsible interfaces.

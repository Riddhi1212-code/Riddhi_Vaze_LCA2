SOCIALINK

Socialink is a social media brand dedicated to crafting unforgettable journeys for wanderlust enthusiasts. This project focused on developing a strong and appealing brand identity, including logo design, visual assets, and branding strategies that reflect the brand’s ethos of adventure, trust, and personalized travel experiences. The creative process involved a deep dive into travel trends, user personas, and the emotional essence of exploring new destinations. The result is a visually striking and cohesive identity that embodies Aarohi’s promise of seamless and inspiring travel, designed to spark curiosity and foster a sense of discovery in every journey.
